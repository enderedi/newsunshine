The Spyder Box

Small power bank and distribution box.

This versatile box is ideal for providing electricity in remote areas, especially when setting up temporary power systems for construction or camping purposes.

Easy to transport and setup, it ensures power distribution is as efficient as possible.

High Voltage Unit

Can power an entire home, community pool, etc.

This unit is designed for large-scale energy needs, providing stable and reliable electricity for industrial and residential use alike.

Its robust design can handle the power requirements of entire homes, office buildings, and more.

Solar Station

Used by anyone working on the tract to power batteries, lights, phones, tools, radios, etc. Max output of 240 volts.

The Solar Station is a portable and efficient solution for powering tools and devices in remote work environments, especially in off-grid locations.

It is the perfect companion for teams in construction, agriculture, or emergency services, providing reliable power when and where it's needed most.
